About

Hi, I'm Sakshi 👋

At Therapy Afterglow, it's all about you. Learning to love yourself, set boundaries, and actually appreciate your own growth. Whether it's personal stuff, substance use, or navigating life as an LGBTQ+ person, every session is your space. You're working for you, not anyone else, and leaving each time feeling seen, supported, and a little more empowered. 💛

I'm a Counselling Psychologist with an MA from MIT World Peace University, Pune, and a background in Social Work. That dual lens means I look at your whole world, not just what's going on in your head. 🌍

I have tattoos, I go to the gym six days a week, I journal, I dance and I genuinely believe therapy works best when your therapist is a real human too. ✨

Why "Afterglow"? Healing doesn't always look dramatic. Sometimes it's quiet. The warmth after a good cry, the exhale after finally saying the thing out loud, the moment you realise you handled something differently. That small inner shift? That's the afterglow. It's what I hope you carry out of every session.

What To Expect

This Is What A Session Feels Like

🫶 No Pressure To Perform

You don't need to arrive composed, articulate or ready with answers. Come as you are, mid-thought, mid-mess, unsure of where to start.

🐢 You Set The Pace

Nothing is pushed. We go at the speed that feels right for you, and you always have the right to redirect, pause or say not yet.

🔬 Evidence-Based, Human-First

I use CBT, person-centred therapy and trauma-informed approaches but I adapt to what works for you, not what works in a textbook.

🔒 A Space That's Entirely Yours

50 minutes where nothing is too small, too strange or too much. What you share here stays here.
